# LoveLiveValues

A compass test for Love Live fans and concert goers, inspired by [SapplyValues](http://sapplypoliticalcompass.com/).

## The Axes

**Degenerate vs. Civilized (X axis)** — How much you glaze over the idols, from full simp to composed appreciator.

**Yakkai vs. Boring (Y axis)** — Your concert behaviour, from ie taiga/mixing/UO-breaking chaos to standing still and barely waving your penlight.

## Privacy

This site is 100% private. No analytics, no cookies, no external requests. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data is ever sent anywhere.
